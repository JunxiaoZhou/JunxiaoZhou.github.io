### Work and Study Experience

- **2025-present** I am currently a tenure-track associate professor at [Nanjing University](https://eng.nju.edu.cn/zjx/main.htm), working in [Prof. Tao Li's group](https://eng.nju.edu.cn/lt/main.htm). My current research focuses on nanophotonics, metasurfaces, optical imaging, edge imaging, quantitative phase imaging, spin-orbit interactions of light, and nonlinear optics. For academic collaboration inquiries, please contact me at [junxiaozhou@nju.edu.cn](mailto:junxiaozhou@nju.edu.cn).
- **2023-2024** I served as a postdoctoral fellow in [Din-ping Tsai's group](https://dinpingtsai.wixsite.com/mysite/refereed-papers) at City University of Hong Kong, where my research focused on nanophotonics, plasmonics, metasurfaces, edge detection, and nonlinear optics.
- **2019-2023** I completed my PhD training at the University of California, San Diego (UCSD), under the guidance of [Zhaowei Liu](https://www.zhaowei.us/), and at Hunan University, under the supervision of [Shuangchun Wen](https://grzy.hnu.edu.cn/site/index/wenshuangchun) and [Hailu Luo](https://grzy.hnu.edu.cn/site/index/luohailu). After my PhD defense, I continued my research as a postdoctoral scholar at UCSD with [Zhaowei Liu](https://www.zhaowei.us/).
